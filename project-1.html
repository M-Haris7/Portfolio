FoodVisor

This End-to-End Machine Learning Project is designed to tackle the challenge of identifying food items and its corresponding calories. This application employs a fine-tuned ResNet model using the Food-101 dataset, achieving an impressive testing accuracy of 77.45%.
The training and testing dataset contains over 101000 images across 100 food items.

Code Link

Project Overview

Recent global studies highlight the importance of a healthy diet for overall wellbeing, with individuals becoming increasingly mindful of their dietary choices. Leveraging scientific advancements, our research introduces a neural network-based model aimed at predicting food items and estimating calorie intake from images. We trained a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) using transfer learning with a dataset of 101,000 images across 101 food categories, achieving 77.45% accuracy. Our system, accessible via a webpage, allows users to upload images for real-time food identification and calorie estimation, showcasing the potential of technology in promoting informed, health-conscious lifestyles.
